# Visualization Task - Middle

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a comprehensive monthly sales trend analysis by combining data from all 12 monthly sales files. Design a 2x2 subplot grid where: (1) Top-left: A line chart showing total monthly revenue with overlaid bar chart showing total units sold per month, (2) Top-right: A stacked area chart displaying the revenue contribution of the top 5 products across all months, (3) Bottom-left: A line chart with error bands showing average order value per month with confidence intervals, and (4) Bottom-right: A dual-axis plot combining a line chart of unique orders count per month with a bar chart showing the number of unique products sold each month. Include proper titles, legends, and annotations highlighting peak and low performance months.
