# Visualization Task - Middle

## Category
Correlation

## Instruction
Create a composite visualization that explores the relationship between economic development and health outcomes across continents. Design a scatter plot showing GDP per capita vs life expectancy for 2007 data, with point sizes representing population and colors representing continents. Overlay trend lines for each continent to highlight different development patterns. Additionally, create a secondary subplot showing the correlation heatmap between GDP per capita, life expectancy, and population across all continents.
