# Visualization Task - Middle

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a comprehensive time series analysis visualization showing the evolution of key Millennium Development Goals indicators across different regions from 1990 to 2014. Design a 2x2 subplot grid where each subplot combines multiple visualization techniques: (1) Top-left: Line plot with confidence bands showing life expectancy trends by region with overlaid scatter points for individual country data, (2) Top-right: Stacked area chart showing the composition of primary education enrollment rates across income groups over time with a secondary y-axis line plot showing global average, (3) Bottom-left: Multi-line plot displaying maternal mortality trends by region with error bars representing data uncertainty, and (4) Bottom-right: Combined bar and line chart showing the relationship between GDP per capita growth (bars) and poverty reduction rates (line) over the MDG period, with different colors representing different regional classifications.
