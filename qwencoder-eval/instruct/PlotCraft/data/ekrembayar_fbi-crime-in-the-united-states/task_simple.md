# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Ranking

## Instruction
Create a horizontal bar chart that ranks the top 15 states by their total violent crime rate per 100,000 inhabitants in 2016. Sort the states from highest to lowest violent crime rate, with the state having the highest rate at the top. Use different colors to distinguish between states above and below the national average violent crime rate. Include proper labels, title, and a legend explaining the color coding.
