# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a line chart showing the progression of total confirmed COVID-19 cases over time for the top 5 most affected states/UTs in India. Use the available daily CSV files to track how confirmed cases evolved from April to August 2020. The chart should display separate lines for each of the top 5 states, with dates on the x-axis and total confirmed cases on the y-axis. Include a legend and appropriate labels.
