# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a line chart showing the progression of total confirmed COVID-19 cases over time for the top 5 most affected states/UTs in India. Use data from at least 10 different date files to show the temporal evolution. The chart should have time on the x-axis and total confirmed cases on the y-axis, with separate colored lines for each of the top 5 states. Include a legend and appropriate title and axis labels.
