# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Distribution

## Instruction
Create a histogram showing the distribution of horse birth years across all Olympic and Paralympic equestrian competitions from Tokyo 2020 and Paris 2024. Use data from all four horse datasets (2020_horses_olympic.csv, 2020_horses_paralympic.csv, 2024_olympic_horses.csv, 2024_paralympic_horses.csv) and display the age distribution with appropriate bins and labels to reveal patterns in horse ages used in elite equestrian competition.

2020_horses_paralympic.csv
2024_olympic_horses.csv
