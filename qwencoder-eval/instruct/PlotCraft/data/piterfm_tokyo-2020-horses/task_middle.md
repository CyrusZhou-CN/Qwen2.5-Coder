# Visualization Task - Middle

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a comprehensive comparison visualization showing the evolution of equestrian participation between Tokyo 2020 and Paris 2024 Olympics. Design a 2x2 subplot grid where: (1) Top-left shows a line chart with markers displaying the change in number of horses by country between the two Olympics, highlighting countries with the most significant increases or decreases, (2) Top-right displays a stacked area chart showing the age distribution evolution of horses from 2020 to 2024, (3) Bottom-left presents a slope chart connecting the gender distribution of horses between the two Olympic years, and (4) Bottom-right shows a dual-axis chart combining bar plots for total participation counts and a line plot for the percentage change in horse participation by discipline/event type between the two years.
