# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Ranking

## Instruction
Create a horizontal bar chart showing the top 10 bowlers ranked by their bowling economy rate (runs conceded per over) in ascending order. Use the bowling_card.csv data to calculate each bowler's economy rate and display their names on the y-axis with economy rates on the x-axis. Add appropriate title, axis labels, and format the chart to clearly highlight which bowlers were most economical with their bowling.
