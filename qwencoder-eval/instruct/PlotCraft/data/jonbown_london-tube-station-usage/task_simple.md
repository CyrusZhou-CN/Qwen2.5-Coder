# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a line chart showing the trend of annual entry/exit totals (in millions) over the years 2007-2017 for the top 5 busiest London Underground stations. Calculate the top 5 stations based on their average annual usage across all years, then plot separate lines for each of these stations to visualize how their passenger traffic evolved over the decade.
