# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Ranking

## Instruction
Create a horizontal bar chart that ranks different animal products by the number of animal lives lost per kilogram (direct deaths only). Sort the products from highest to lowest number of lives lost per kg, and use different colors to distinguish between different types of animal products. Add appropriate labels and title to clearly show which animal products require the most animal lives per kilogram of food produced.
