## Instruction
Create a composite visualization analyzing the distribution of article topics in the RNA protein dataset. Generate a 2x1 subplot layout where the top plot shows a histogram with overlaid KDE curve displaying the frequency distribution of unique article titles across both Portuguese and English datasets, and the bottom plot presents a violin plot comparing the text length distributions between Portuguese and English articles. Include proper titles, axis labels, and a legend to distinguish between the two languages.
