## Category
Distribution

## Instruction
Create a histogram showing the distribution of text lengths (measured in number of characters) for the Portuguese RNA articles dataset. Calculate the character count for each article in the 'Texto' column, then create a histogram with appropriate bins to visualize how the article lengths are distributed. Include proper axis labels, a title, and add a vertical line showing the mean text length with an annotation displaying the mean value.
