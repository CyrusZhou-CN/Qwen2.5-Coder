# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a line chart showing the evolution of cryptocurrency midpoint prices over time for all three cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, ADA) using their 5-minute interval data. Plot time on the x-axis and midpoint price on the y-axis, with separate colored lines for each cryptocurrency. Include a legend to distinguish between the three cryptocurrencies and format the y-axis to show appropriate price scales for each currency.
