# Visualization Task - Middle

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a comprehensive time-series analysis visualization showing the evolution of the blockchain job market from 2018-2019. Design a 2x2 subplot grid where each subplot combines multiple visualization techniques: (1) Top-left: A line chart showing monthly job posting counts overlaid with a bar chart showing average salary ranges by month, (2) Top-right: An area chart displaying the cumulative growth of different job categories (technical vs non-technical roles) over time with trend lines, (3) Bottom-left: A stacked area chart showing the composition of job locations over time with a secondary line plot indicating remote work percentage, (4) Bottom-right: A combination of scatter plot and line chart showing the relationship between company funding levels and job posting frequency over time, with different colors representing company size categories.
