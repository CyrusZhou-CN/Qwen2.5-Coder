# Visualization Task - Middle

## Category
Ranking

## Instruction
Create a comprehensive ranking visualization that compares the top blockchain/crypto companies by their hiring activity and compensation patterns. Design a 2x2 subplot grid where: (1) Top-left: A horizontal bar chart showing the top 15 companies ranked by total number of job postings, with bars colored by average salary range midpoint; (2) Top-right: A lollipop chart displaying the top 10 companies with the highest average salary offerings, with point sizes representing the number of job postings; (3) Bottom-left: A stacked horizontal bar chart showing the top 12 companies by job count, with each bar segmented by job category (technical vs non-technical roles based on tags); (4) Bottom-right: A dot plot comparing company funding levels (from companies.csv) against their job posting activity, with companies ranked by funding amount and dot colors indicating employee count ranges.
