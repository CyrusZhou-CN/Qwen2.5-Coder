# Visualization Task - Hard

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a comprehensive 2x2 subplot grid analyzing temporal trading patterns in the Nepal Stock Exchange data. Each subplot should be a composite visualization combining multiple chart types:

Top-left: Create a dual-axis plot showing daily trading volume (bar chart) overlaid with a moving average trend line of stock prices across time for the top 5 most actively traded companies.

Top-right: Generate a time series heatmap showing trading intensity by hour of day and day of week, combined with box plots on the margins showing the distribution of trade amounts for different time periods.

Bottom-left: Construct a multi-layered area chart displaying the cumulative trading volume over time for different stock sectors, with individual trend lines for each major company overlaid on top.

Bottom-right: Design a composite plot combining a time series of price volatility (calculated as rolling standard deviation) with scatter points sized by trading volume, and add error bands showing confidence intervals around the volatility trend.

Use consistent color schemes across all subplots and ensure proper temporal axis alignment. Include appropriate legends and annotations highlighting significant trading events or patterns.
