# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Distribution

## Instruction
Create a histogram showing the distribution of stock trading rates (prices) across all companies in the Nepal Stock Exchange dataset. Use the 'Rate' column from multiple CSV files to analyze the overall price distribution pattern. Include proper binning to reveal the shape of the distribution, add a kernel density estimate overlay to smooth the distribution curve, and provide appropriate labels and title. The visualization should help identify whether stock prices follow a normal distribution, are right-skewed, or show any other interesting distributional characteristics.
