# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a line chart showing the evolution of closing prices over time for three major US stock indices: S&P 500 (^GSPC), NASDAQ Composite (^IXIC), and Dow Jones Industrial Average (^DJI). Use data from 2008-2023 to visualize how these indices recovered and grew following the 2008 financial crisis. Plot all three indices on the same chart with different colors and include a legend. Add appropriate title and axis labels to clearly show the long-term trend patterns.
