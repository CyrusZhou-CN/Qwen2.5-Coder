# Visualization Task - Middle

## Category
Composition

## Instruction
Create a composite visualization showing the composition of home renovation preferences across different aspects. Design a 2x2 subplot grid where: (1) Top-left: A stacked bar chart showing the distribution of renovation motivations by house type, (2) Top-right: A pie chart displaying the composition of preferred rooms/parts to renovate, (3) Bottom-left: A stacked bar chart showing budget ranges by neighborhood type, and (4) Bottom-right: A horizontal bar chart showing the composition of valued home decor aspects. Use different color palettes for each subplot and ensure proper titles and legends.
