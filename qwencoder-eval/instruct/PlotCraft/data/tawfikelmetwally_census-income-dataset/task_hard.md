# Visualization Task - Hard

## Category
Distribution

## Instruction
Create a comprehensive 3x3 subplot grid analyzing the distribution patterns of key demographic and economic variables in the census income dataset. Each subplot should combine multiple visualization techniques: (1) Top-left: Age distribution with both histogram and KDE overlay, colored by income level; (2) Top-center: Education years distribution with violin plot and box plot overlay, split by gender; (3) Top-right: Hours per week distribution with histogram and KDE, faceted by workclass; (4) Middle-left: Capital gain distribution with log-scale histogram and KDE for non-zero values only; (5) Middle-center: Marital status distribution with stacked bar chart showing income proportions and overlaid count labels; (6) Middle-right: Race distribution with horizontal bar chart and percentage annotations, colored by income level; (7) Bottom-left: Occupation distribution with rotated bar chart and density rug plot on secondary axis; (8) Bottom-center: Native country distribution (top 10 countries) with lollipop chart and income ratio annotations; (9) Bottom-right: Combined capital gain vs capital loss scatter plot with marginal histograms and income-based color coding.
