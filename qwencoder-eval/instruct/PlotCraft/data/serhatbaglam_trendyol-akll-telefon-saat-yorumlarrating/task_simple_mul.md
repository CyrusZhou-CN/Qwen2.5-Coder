# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Ranking

## Instruction
Create a horizontal bar chart that ranks smartphone and smartwatch brands by their average customer ratings. Calculate the mean rating for each brand across all their products, then display the brands in descending order from highest to lowest average rating. Include the exact average rating values as text labels on each bar, and use different colors to distinguish between smartphone brands and smartwatch brands.
