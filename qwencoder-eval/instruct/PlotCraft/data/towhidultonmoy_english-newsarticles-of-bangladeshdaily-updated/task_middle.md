# Visualization Task - Middle

## Category
Change

## Instruction
Create a comprehensive time-series analysis visualization showing the evolution of news publication patterns across different Bangladeshi English newspapers. Design a 2x2 subplot grid where: (1) Top-left: A line chart with markers showing daily article counts over time for each publisher, with different colors and line styles for each newspaper; (2) Top-right: A stacked area chart displaying the cumulative proportion of articles published by each newspaper over the time period; (3) Bottom-left: A bar chart showing the total article count per publisher with a secondary y-axis line plot overlay indicating the average article length (character count) per publisher; (4) Bottom-right: A heatmap showing publication activity by day of the week and hour of the day (extracted from news_collection_time), with color intensity representing article frequency. Include proper legends, titles, and annotations highlighting peak publication periods and notable trends in the data.
