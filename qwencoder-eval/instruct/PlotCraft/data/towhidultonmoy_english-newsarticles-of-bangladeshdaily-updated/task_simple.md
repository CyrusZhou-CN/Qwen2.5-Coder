# Visualization Task - Simple

## Category
Composition

## Instruction
Create a pie chart showing the distribution of news articles across different Bangladeshi English newspapers. Use the combined dataset to calculate the percentage contribution of each publisher (thedailystar, thefinancialexpress, newagebd, daily-sun) to the total news collection. Display the percentages on each slice and use distinct colors for each publisher. Add a title "Distribution of News Articles by Publisher" and ensure the chart clearly shows which newspaper contributes the most to the dataset.
